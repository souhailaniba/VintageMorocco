Get to know us, what we do and why we do it!!

Who?

Our team is made up of 3 computer engineering students who are passionate about vintage Moroccan items and want to share their love for these unique and beautiful items with the world.

What?

Vintage Morocco sells a variety of rare vintage Moroccan items, such as furniture, decor, clothing, and accessories. The items are chosen for their quality, uniqueness, and cultural significance.

Why?

Our team is passionate about preserving Moroccan culture by sharing rare vintage items with the world, believing that they represent the country's rich history and cultural heritage that must be kept alive for future generations.
